Tonto National Monument

Known for its well-preserved cliff dwellings that provide a glimpse into the lives of the Salado culture from the 13th to 15th centuries.

Canyonlands National Park

Known for its dramatic desert landscape, featuring intricate canyons, towering mesas, and unique rock formations carved by the Colorado River and its tributaries.

San Antonio Missions National Historical Park

Known for preserving four of the five Spanish frontier missions, offering insight into the cultural interchanges between indigenous peoples and Spanish settlers in the 18th century.
